1 : √2 : √3 — the cube's "lost triangle"

Construction sequence: two unit edges make a face diagonal √2; lift one unit vertically; the diagonal through the cube becomes √3.

Measurements
unit edge = 1
face diagonal = √2
space diagonal = √3
proof triangle = 1 · √2 · √3
exact angle = 35.264°
Drag to orbit · scroll to zoom

Proof: face plane: 1² + 1² = 2 → face diagonal √2. Then vertical lift: (√2)² + 1² = 3 → cube diagonal √3.
Angles: θ = atan(1/√2) = 35.264°; complement = 54.736°. The plan projection of the face diagonal is 45° to each unit edge.
