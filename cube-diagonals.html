Unit Cube Diagonal Geometry

The construction explores three fundamental lengths inside a unit cube (edge = 1):

edge = 1 · face diagonal = √2 · space diagonal = √3

The green triangle shows the key structural relationship: a right triangle with legs 1 and √2, hypotenuse √3.

1² + (√2)² = 1 + 2 = 3 = (√3)²

This produces the characteristic angles: arctan(1/√2) ≈ 35.264°, complement ≈ 54.736°. These proportions form the geometric DNA of Gregg Fleishman's modular, interlocking structures.
